# Penguins of the Southern Ocean

## Emperor penguins

The emperor penguin is the tallest and heaviest of all living penguin species.
Adults reach about 1.2 meters and breed on the Antarctic sea ice through the
polar winter. Males incubate the single egg on their feet for roughly 65 days.

## Diet and foraging

Penguins eat krill, fish, and squid. Emperor penguins dive deeper than any other
bird, reaching depths beyond 500 meters in search of food. A dive can last more
than 20 minutes.

## Conservation status code

The reference identifier for the emperor penguin monitoring program is PEN-2026-EMP.
That exact code, PEN-2026-EMP, is used in every field report and is the term a
lexical search must catch even when no semantic neighbor exists.
